The elegant image of a woman is created in a fantastical poetic bridal possibility...
On October 17, 1998, at Moulin Rouge in Montmartre, a celebration of the wedding dress is unveiled with a humorous touch.

Love, life, and death, a combination of fashion spectacle and bridal rituals.

A exploration of gender roles and the magic of a dress underneath a dress, relishing in the joy of dressing.

The inclusion of playful dimensions, exquisite tailoring, lots of layering, and white shirts turned into wedding dresses, crinolines, top hats, even puffers.

"Behind the wedding dress, there must be many stories", said Yohji Yamamoto of the collection. "[lt] is burdened by the drama of one's life".
